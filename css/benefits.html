BENEFITS
     Playing battle royale games:
  • Increases our concentration
  • Acts as a stressbuster
  • Increases our knowledge of guns and weapons.
  • Trains people to make the right decisions faster without loss of accuracy.
  • Develops a heightened sensitivity to what is going around
  • Helps with multitasking, driving, keeping track of friends in crowd, and navigating around
  • Helps to overcome dyslexia and helps to improve Visual Skills
  • Develop children's cognitive skills.
